## Personal Introduction

![](https://image.honahec.cc/avatar.png){width="40%"}

The name Honahec was created by rearranging the pinyin of my real name (making it quite unique, I suppose).

I think my learning journey has been quite interesting.

**2020, 8th Grade** Through some coincidences, I joined my school's informatics olympiad team and started learning programming. However, I had no clear goals at that time, and my participation in CSP-J that year didn't yield any notable results.

**2021, 9th Grade** This year I directly participated in CSP-S and won a provincial first prize (perhaps with some luck). In the same year, I participated in NOIP and achieved a score above the provincial second prize cutoff (junior high students don't receive certificates).

**2022, 10th Grade** As everyone knows, informatics olympiad students eventually switch tracks (I started studying physics olympiad). Perhaps the previous year's success made me a bit overconfident, as I foolishly attempted to participate in both competitions simultaneously. This inevitably became a regret.

**2023, 11th Grade** This year I decided to focus on physics olympiad (though choosing informatics might have been the more rational decision). I won a provincial first prize in physics olympiad that year (finally made it through).

**2024, 12th Grade** Now, my main focus is naturally on the college entrance exam, but given my personality, I can still work on projects that need to be done (it's impossible to study all the time).

The story continues. Let me quote a line from my [home page](https://honahec.cc):

**The road ahead is long, but don't forget the starlight behind you**
